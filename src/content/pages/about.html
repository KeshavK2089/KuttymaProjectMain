About Kuttyma

Kuttyma Inc. was founded in 2024 by siblings Keshav and Srinidhi Kotteswaran after seeing artisans in Coimbatore struggle to sell online. Our mission is to democratize digital commerce for India's 63 million small businesses with affordable, fully managed websites.

  • Accessibility – making digital tools affordable
  • Empowerment – enabling entrepreneurs to grow online
  • Cultural Sensitivity – respecting local languages and customs
  • Continuous Support – 24/7 WhatsApp assistance
  • Innovation – practical tech for real business problems
Helping small businesses stay connected in an ever changing technological landscape. Keshav Kotteswaran Digital Transformation Consultant
